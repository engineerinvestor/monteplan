# Case Studies

Real-world financial planning scenarios analyzed with monteplan. Each case study defines a persona, explores key decisions, and provides actionable takeaways.

## Interactive Notebooks

For hands-on exploration, see the [Notebooks](../notebooks/index.md) section. The FIRE and Retiree case studies are available as runnable notebooks.

## Case Studies

### [FIRE at 45](fire.md)

Alex, 30, aggressive saver. Can early retirement at 45 work with $3,500/month spending? Explores the impact of spending levels, retirement age, spending policies, and market crashes on a 45-year retirement.

### [Coast FIRE](coast-fire.md)

Jamie, 35, has $480K saved and wants to stop contributing entirely. How far can compound growth carry the portfolio to a traditional retirement at 60?

### [Conservative Retiree](conservative-retiree.md)

Pat, 60, nearing traditional retirement with $700K and Social Security. Examines the outsized impact of guaranteed income, glide paths, tax model choice, and inflation sensitivity.

### [Guardrails vs Constant Spending](guardrails-vs-constant.md)

A head-to-head comparison of the two most popular spending strategies. When do guardrails justify their added complexity?
